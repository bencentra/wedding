FAQ

Is there a COVID-19 vaccine requirement?

We are requiring that all guests who are able to have received a COVID-19 vaccine. In addition, we would prefer that guests are also up to date on their booster shots. Finally, please comply with the masking rules of any of our venues. For more information, visit the Massachusetts COVID-19 website.

Can I bring a +1?

Of course, significant others are welcome! Please include them when you fill out the RSVP form so we can plan accordingly.

Can I bring my children?

Yes! Children are also welcome, though unfortunately they won't be able to enjoy any beer. We'll have some games on the patio, as well as a quiet corner of the brewery for them to relax in.

I'm gonna take lots of photos! How do I share them with you?

The best way to share your photos with us is to add them to this Google Drive folder. You can also share them on social media with the hashtag #benjauren2022 so that we can find them later. We won't have a professional photographer at the party, so please take lots of photos!

Is there a dress code for any of the events?

Nope! Please do wear clothes though.

I don't actually like beer. Will there be other drinks?

Of course! Lord Hobo also serves wine and has a guest cider on tap. There will be no spirits or cocktails at Lord Hobo. To get an idea for what will be on tap, check out the taproom website.

Can I pet your dog?

Unfortunately, Dusty will be boarded the whole weekend. You can always look at like a million photos of him on Google Photos or follow him on Instagram.
